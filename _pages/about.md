Hi, I’m **Prashant Rawat**.

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at Dalhousie University, working in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

My research focuses on **multimodal interaction in Virtual Reality**, exploring how **hand, voice, and gaze input** support **spatial thinking in 3D design tasks**.

I design and evaluate interactive systems that help users better understand spatial relationships, plan actions, and reduce cognitive load in immersive environments.

## Research Focus

- Multimodal interaction (hand, voice, gaze)

## Research Statement

My research aims to bridge human spatial thinking and interactive system design by developing multimodal interfaces that align with natural human actions.

I focus on designing systems that not only improve task performance but also support cognitive processes such as planning, visualization, and relational reasoning in immersive environments.
